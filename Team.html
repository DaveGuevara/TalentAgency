OUR TEAM

These extraordinary women founded ATG International. Their dedication and hard work is noticeable in all aspects of ATG's business culture, and their passion for the entertainment industry brought them together to achieve a level of excellence in the field.


Diana Carolina Guevara | COO |

Her grandfather being a professional songwriter and producer and her uncle an actor, Carolina always had a heart for the arts. With a background in marketing and communications, Carolina began her career working in the music industry in 2007. Developing her skills in the planning, development, and logistics aspect of a show, she quickly learned her passion lay in seeing and impacting the evolution of an individual’s project. For years, she had the honor to work with renown artists such as Vicente Fernandez, Romeo Santos, and Chayanne. Eventually, establishing a partnership with CEO and founder of ATG International, Yarit Acuff.

Yarit Acuff | CEO |

Adriana Cristina Rodriguez

The liason between ATG International and South America. Years of experience in entertainment production, theatre, television and film. Graduated from "Pontificia Universidad Javeriana" with a degree in Creative Arts. Through the years she as worked in scene production as well as national and international theater. Her passion lays in the formation and development of local artists.

Together, along with a team of exceptional individuals, these women form ATG International, a group focused on making a mark in the field of entertainment, creating the crossover and expansion of the talent that will impact today’s industry.
